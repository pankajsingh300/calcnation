Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here's how we handle your data.

No Data Collection

CalcNation does not collect, store, or sell any personal data. Period.

How Our Tools Work

All processing happens in your browser on your device:

  • PDF operations (compress, merge, split, convert) run locally using JavaScript
  • File conversions (JSON, Base64, regex testing) happen in-browser
  • Calculations (mortgage, EMI, finance) compute in your browser
  • Files you upload are never sent to any server
  • Your input data disappears when you refresh the page

Browser Storage

We use localStorage only to remember your currency preference. This is stored locally on your device and can be cleared anytime by clearing your browser's site data.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand which tools are most popular and improve the site. Google Analytics does not identify you personally—it only tracks page views, time on site, and which tools get used.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by using a browser extension like Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Third-Party Libraries

CalcNation uses:

  • pdf-lib.js for PDF manipulation (open-source, runs in browser)
  • pdfjs for PDF parsing (open-source, runs in browser)
  • jszip for file compression (open-source, runs in browser)

All of these are open-source libraries that process data locally. No data is sent externally.

Cookies

CalcNation does not use cookies. We use localStorage for your currency preference only.

External Links

CalcNation pages may link to external sites (GitHub, documentation, etc.). We're not responsible for their privacy policies. Always review their policies before sharing personal data.

Security

CalcNation is served over HTTPS. Your browser-device connection is encrypted.

Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there's nothing to delete, modify, or export. Your data is entirely in your control.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Contact us.

Policy Changes

We'll update this policy if our practices change. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
