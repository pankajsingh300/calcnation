// Frequently Asked Questions

What is a mortgage calculator? +
A mortgage calculator helps you estimate your monthly home loan payments based on the loan amount, interest rate, loan term, and down payment.
What is the formula for mortgage calculation? +
Monthly Payment = P × r × (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n - 1), where P is the loan principal (home value minus down payment), r is the monthly interest rate, and n is the total number of payments.
What is an amortization schedule? +
An amortization schedule shows each monthly payment broken down into principal and interest portions, and the remaining loan balance after each payment.
How much down payment do I need? +
Typically lenders require 10–20% down payment. A higher down payment reduces your monthly EMI and total interest paid over the loan term.
Is this mortgage calculator free? +
Yes, completely free. No registration required. All calculations happen instantly in your browser with no data sent to any server.
