About CalcNation

Free, privacy-first tools for developers, QA engineers, and automation professionals.

Our Mission

CalcNation exists to provide free, instant, reliable tools that developers and QA engineers need every day. No signup. No tracking. No ads. Just useful tools.

What We Offer

  • PDF Tools: Compress, merge, split, convert PDFs

  • Automation Tools: Test data generators, selector inspectors, API mocks

  • Blog & Articles: Automation testing guides, best practices, tech tips
