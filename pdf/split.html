Frequently Asked Questions

Can I extract specific pages from a PDF? ▾
Yes. Use the "By Page Range" mode and enter the exact pages or ranges you want. For example, entering "2, 5-8, 12" will extract pages 2, 5, 6, 7, 8, and 12 into one PDF.
How many files will be created when I split? ▾
It depends on your settings. Each comma-separated range creates one file. "Split every N pages" creates one file per chunk. "Extract all" creates one file per page.
Are the split PDFs uploaded anywhere? ▾
No. All processing happens in your browser using JavaScript. Your PDF and the resulting files never leave your device.
