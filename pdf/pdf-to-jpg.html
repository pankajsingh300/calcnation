Frequently Asked Questions

What DPI should I choose? ▾
For screen viewing, 72-108 DPI is sufficient. For printing or sharing professionally, choose 144 DPI or higher. Higher DPI creates larger, sharper images but takes more time to process.
JPG or PNG — which is better? ▾
JPG is better for photos and colorful content — smaller file size. PNG is better for documents with text, as it's lossless and preserves sharp edges on letters.
How many pages can I convert? ▾
All pages in your PDF will be converted. For very large PDFs (100+ pages), the process may take a minute or two depending on your device's speed.
