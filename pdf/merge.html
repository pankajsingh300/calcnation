Frequently Asked Questions

How many PDFs can I merge at once? ▾
You can merge unlimited PDF files at once. There's no restriction on the number of files. However, very large files may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities.
Will the merged PDF have watermarks? ▾
No watermarks, ever. Your merged PDF will be clean and identical to what you'd get from a paid tool — completely free.
Can I change the page order? ▾
Yes! After uploading, you can drag and drop the files to reorder them. The PDFs will be merged in the exact order shown in the list.
Is the merged file uploaded anywhere? ▾
No. Everything happens in your browser. Your files never leave your device. This is completely private and secure — no data is ever transmitted.
