What Makes a Strong Password?

  • At least 12 characters (16+ is better)
  • Mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
  • Numbers (0-9)
  • Special characters (!@#$%^&*)
  • No personal information
  • No common dictionary words
  • No sequential patterns

Password Security Statistics

  • Average hacking time for 6-char password: 10 seconds
  • Average hacking time for 12-char password: 5 years
  • Average hacking time for 16-char password: 300 years

Password Manager Tips

  • Use 1Password, LastPass, or KeePass to store passwords
  • Never reuse passwords across sites
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Change passwords if data breach occurs

  • Using your name, DOB, or pet name
  • Using "123456" or "password"
  • Writing passwords down on sticky notes
  • Telling others your password
  • Using same password for multiple sites
