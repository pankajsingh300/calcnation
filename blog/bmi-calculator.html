BMI Formula

BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ Height (m)²

BMI Categories

Range Category
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Normal weight
25 - 29.9 Overweight
30+ Obese

Limitations of BMI

  • Doesn't account for muscle vs fat
  • Different for different ages
  • Different standards for adults/children
  • Should be combined with other health metrics
