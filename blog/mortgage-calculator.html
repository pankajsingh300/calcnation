Mortgage Calculator: Calculate Home Loan Payments

Buying a home is a major financial decision. Understand your mortgage payments before committing with our mortgage calculator.

Mortgage Components

  • Principal: Actual home price minus down payment
  • Interest: Cost of borrowing the money
  • Property Tax: Annual tax on property value
  • Insurance: Homeowner's insurance premiums
  • HOA Fees: Community maintenance fees (if applicable)

Common Mortgage Terms

  • 15-year mortgage: Higher payment, less interest paid
  • 30-year mortgage: Lower payment, more interest paid
  • Fixed rate: Same rate for entire loan term
  • ARM: Rate adjusts after initial period

Down Payment Impact

  • 20% down: Avoid PMI (mortgage insurance)
  • 10-20% down: Need PMI (adds to payment)
  • Less than 10% down: Higher interest rates

Mortgage Tips

  • Get pre-approved before house hunting
  • Shop rates from multiple lenders
  • Consider making extra payments to build equity faster
  • Refinance if rates drop significantly
